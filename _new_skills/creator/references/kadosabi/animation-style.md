# Kadosabi Animation Style

Use this reference for the base Kadosabi visual system.

## Style Overview

Create a high-quality 3D render in a minimalist, stylized character design aesthetic. The style should resemble premium toy-like 3D characters with clean geometry, soft materials, and studio lighting. The aesthetic should feel like Pixar-level polish blended with Apple product rendering: simple, friendly, and controlled.

## Character Design

- Slightly exaggerated proportions with a larger head and smaller body.
- Rounded, simplified forms.
- No sharp edges; use smooth bevels.
- Simplified mitten-like hands.
- Minimal facial features:
  - small oval or dot eyes, solid color
  - subtle curved mouth
  - no eyelashes, detailed nose, or realism

## Materials

- Soft matte to satin finishes.
- Skin: smooth, clay-like material with subtle shading.
- Clothing: soft, slightly rubberized cotton feel.
- Shoes: structured but soft, combining matte leather and foam-like materials.
- Avoid glossy or reflective surfaces.
- Avoid noisy or complex texture maps.

## Lighting

- Large soft key light.
- Gentle fill light.
- Subtle rim light for separation.
- Soft diffused shadows.
- No dramatic contrast or harsh lighting.

## Color

- Restrained, curated palette.
- Slightly desaturated but still rich.
- Avoid overly vibrant or clashing colors.

## Clothing

- Simplified real-world clothing.
- Minimal seams and stitching.
- Broad intentional folds, not realistic fabric simulation.

## Panda-Style Sneakers

- Black and white low-top sneaker design inspired by Nike Dunk Low "Panda."
- Clean color blocking: white base with black overlays.
- Simplified details.
- Chunky laces with smooth cylindrical form.
- Minimal stylized perforated toe box.
- Thick sole with soft edges.
- Branding minimal or generic; avoid explicit logos.

## Rendering

- High resolution.
- Clean edges with strong anti-aliasing.
- No grain or noise.
- Pure or light neutral background unless scene requires otherwise.

## Composition

- Full-body or product-style framing.
- Slight perspective angle.
- Centered or balanced composition.

## Do Not

- Add unnecessary detail.
- Introduce realism in textures or anatomy.
- Break cohesion across elements.
