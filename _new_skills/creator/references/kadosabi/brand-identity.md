# kadosabi: brand identity

## what it is

lifestyle brand. street art vibes, home goods, rubber hose animation—all one universe. the bridge between nostalgic 1930s aesthetics and sharp cyberpunk edges.

## the name

- **kado** (角) = edge, angle. sharp.
- **sabi** (寂) = from wabi-sabi. beauty in things that are broken or aging.
- **the narrative:** the beautiful edge.

| script | kanji | meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| カドサビ | 角寂 | elegant edge |

> katakana preferred in the cyberpunk context for a modern "tech-street" feel.

## visual identity

**palette:** high-contrast monochromatic. black and white as primary. color is used deliberately as signal, not decoration.

**aesthetic:** the bridge between 1930s rubber hose animation and sharp cyberpunk edges.

## character universe

the full character + world spec lives in [character-bible.md](character-bible.md).

- **wyatt bose** — the protagonist. cardboard box head, 1930s rubber hose physics.
- **benny bounce** — best friend. soft, bouncy, maximum secondary motion.
- **lola lumina** — lead. 1930s femme fatale meets 1990s nyc goth.
- **void** — the villain. pure black circle. erases color from the world.
