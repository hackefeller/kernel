# Lucy Character Reference

Use this reference when generating Lucy.

## Identity

Lucy is a stylized Puerto Rican female character in a minimalist toy-like aesthetic with a gritty urban pop-art edge.

## Style System

- Premium toy + urban grit.
- Simplified, rounded, slightly exaggerated geometry.
- No sharp edges; use soft bevels.
- Slightly larger head, slim but defined body.

## Facial Design

- Solid oval eyes, no sclera, no realism.
- Simple expressive eyebrows.
- Minimal or softly implied nose.
- Small subtle mouth curve.
- Expression should come from pose and tilt, not facial complexity.

## Features

- Warm medium tan skin tone.
- Curly or wavy dark hair in a high messy bun, with loose strands framing the face.
- Gold hoop earrings.
- Confident, relaxed expression.
- Feminine but grounded presence.
- Avoid stereotypes or caricature.

## Hair

- Textured chunky stylized curls, not individual strands.
- Matte to soft satin finish.
- Clean silhouette with slight asymmetry.

## Clothing

- White cropped tank top.
- Black joggers with simplified folds.
- Small Puerto Rican flag patch on upper thigh.
- Panda-style black and white low-top sneakers.

## Pose

- Relaxed, confident stance.
- Slight hip shift or lean.
- One arm resting casually when appropriate.

## Environment

- Gritty urban alley with graffiti, posters, and stickers.
- Wet pavement with soft reflections.
- Grime and texture belong in the environment, not on the character materials.

## Color And Rendering

- Restrained black, white, and neutral palette.
- One accent allowed: Puerto Rican flag patch.
- Slight desaturation.
- High resolution.
- Clean anti-aliasing.
- Balanced slightly cinematic angle.

## Do Not

- Add hyper-real skin textures.
- Add excessive detail.
- Use harsh lighting or deep shadows.
- Break simplified rounded design language.
