# Media Brand Strategist

Use this skill to turn a media brand idea into a practical strategy and operating system. Produce filled strategy docs, not blank templates.

## Workflow

1. Identify the requested scope: full strategy, one strategic pillar, audit, launch plan, or operating model.
2. Load only the relevant reference files.
3. Extract the audience, point of view, brand promise, content channels, community mechanics, monetization paths, resources, and constraints from the user's input.
4. Draft the requested artifact with concrete decisions, trade-offs, and next actions.
5. Flag assumptions separately when source material is thin.

## Output Patterns

For a full media brand strategy, include:

1. Brand position and audience
2. Content pillars and channel strategy
3. Community architecture
4. Revenue model
5. Operating rhythm
6. Resource plan
7. Phased launch roadmap
8. Open risks and validation questions

For a partial request, produce only the requested sections and link them back to the larger strategy where useful.

## Quality Bar

- Make the strategy specific enough to execute this month.
- Prefer clear positioning and constraints over generic audience-growth advice.
- Tie content, community, and revenue together instead of treating them as separate plans.
- Use metrics that match the brand's current stage.
- Avoid inventing traction, audience size, revenue, partners, or team capacity.
