## 6.1 Revenue Streams

### Tier 1: Audience Monetization

**Advertising & Sponsorships:**

- YouTube AdSense: $3-8 CPM
- Brand sponsorships: $5,000-50,000 per integration
- Affiliate commissions: 5-20% of sales
- Platform creator funds (TikTok, Instagram)

**Revenue Potential:** $5,000-50,000/month at scale

**Requirements:**

- Consistent content production
- Substantial audience (100k+ followers)
- High engagement rates
- Brand-safe content

### Tier 2: Product Monetization

**Digital Products:**

_Entry Products ($10-50):_

- Style guides and PDFs
- Template packs
- Curated lists and recommendations
- Monthly content subscriptions

_Core Products ($100-500):_

- Comprehensive courses
- Premium playbooks
- Assessment and planning tools
- Annual memberships

_Premium Products ($500-2,000):_

- Advanced certification programs
- Masterclass series
- Complete systems and frameworks
- Lifetime access packages

**Physical Products:**

- Books ($20-40)
- Workbooks and journals ($25-50)
- Merchandise ($30-150)
- Curated product boxes ($100-300)

**Revenue Potential:** $25,000-250,000/month at scale

### Tier 3: Service Monetization

**Community Memberships:**

- Free tier: Lead generation
- Basic tier: $25-50/month
- Premium tier: $100-200/month
- Elite tier: $500-1,000/month or application-only

**Individual Services:**

- One-time consultations: $500-2,000
- Wardrobe audits: $1,500-5,000
- Personal shopping: $2,000-10,000
- Ongoing advisory: $2,000-5,000/month

**Group Programs:**

- Mastermind groups: $500-1,500/month
- Cohort-based courses: $997-2,997
- Workshops and retreats: $1,000-5,000

**Revenue Potential:** $50,000-500,000/month at scale

### Tier 4: IP & Partnerships

**Brand Collaborations:**

- Product line collaborations: $50,000-500,000+
- Content partnerships: $10,000-100,000
- Affiliate arrangements: Commission-based
- Ambassador programs: Retainer + commission

**B2B Services:**

- Corporate training: $10,000-50,000 per engagement
- Executive coaching: $5,000-15,000/month per client
- Brand consulting: $15,000-100,000 per project
- Content licensing: $5,000-50,000

**Media & Speaking:**

- Conference keynotes: $10,000-50,000
- Workshop facilitation: $15,000-75,000
- Book advances: $50,000-500,000+
- Media appearances: Varies + brand exposure

**Revenue Potential:** $100,000-1,000,000+ annually

## 6.2 Pricing Strategy

### Value-Based Pricing Framework

**Entry Products:**

- Price: $10-50
- Value perception: "No-brainer" offer
- Conversion rate: 5-15%
- Primary goal: List building and qualification

**Core Products:**

- Price: $100-500
- Value perception: "Significant investment, clear ROI"
- Conversion rate: 2-5%
- Primary goal: Revenue generation and customer development

**Premium Services:**

- Price: $1,000-10,000+
- Value perception: "Transformation and status"
- Conversion rate: 0.5-2%
- Primary goal: High-value relationships and profits

### Price Points by Product Type

**Digital Products:**

- PDF guides: $29-49
- Video courses: $97-497
- Comprehensive programs: $497-1,997
- Masterclasses: $297-997

**Services:**

- Community (monthly): $25-200
- Group coaching (monthly): $500-1,500
- 1-on-1 consulting (per session): $500-2,000
- Retainer services (monthly): $2,000-10,000

**Physical Products:**

- Paperback books: $19-29
- Hardcover books: $29-49
- Premium editions: $75-150
- Product boxes: $100-300

## 6.4 Sales Funnels

### Awareness to Purchase Flow

**Stage 1: Awareness**

- Channel: Social media content
- Goal: Attention and interest
- Metric: Reach and impressions

**Stage 2: Interest**

- Channel: Lead magnet, free community
- Goal: Email capture or community join
- Metric: Conversion rate

**Stage 3: Consideration**

- Channel: Email nurture, community engagement
- Goal: Education and trust building
- Metric: Engagement rate

**Stage 4: Purchase**

- Channel: Sales page, application
- Goal: First transaction
- Metric: Conversion rate, AOV

**Stage 5: Retention**

- Channel: Product delivery, community
- Goal: Satisfaction and repeat purchase
- Metric: Retention rate, LTV

### Primary Funnels

**Content → Community → Course:**

1. Viral content piece
2. CTA to join free community
3. Onboarding sequence
4. Course pitch to qualified members
5. Upsell to premium membership

**Lead Magnet → Email → Product:**

1. Valuable free guide
2. Email capture
3. Automated email sequence (5-7 emails)
4. Product offer
5. Order bump and upsell

**Free Community → Paid Membership:**

1. Join free Discord/community
2. Experience value and culture
3. See premium member benefits
4. Limited-time offer or application
5. Convert to paid tier

**Social → Consultation → Retainer:**

1. High-value content demonstration
2. DM or inquiry
3. Discovery call
4. One-time consultation
5. Retainer proposal

## 6.5 Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Organic Channels:**

- Content marketing: $10-30 per lead
- Community building: $5-15 per member
- Referrals: $5-20 per customer

**Paid Channels:**

- Social media ads: $30-100 per customer
- Influencer partnerships: $50-150 per customer
- Search ads: $75-200 per customer

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Free Community Member:**

- Average LTV: $50-200
- Conversion to paid: 5-10%
- Time to conversion: 3-6 months

**Paid Community Member:**

- Average LTV: $600-2,400
- Average membership length: 12-24 months
- Upsell rate: 15-30%

**Course Customer:**

- Average LTV: $200-500
- Repeat purchase rate: 30-50%
- Time to repeat: 6-12 months

**Coaching Client:**

- Average LTV: $5,000-25,000
- Average engagement: 3-12 months
- Referral rate: 40-60%

### Target Ratios

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** 3:1 minimum, 5:1 optimal
**Payback Period:** 6 months or less
**Gross Margin:** 70%+ on digital, 40%+ on services
**Operating Margin:** 20%+ at scale

**One-Time Payments:**

- Courses and products
- Workshops and events
- Consulting sessions
- Books and merchandise

**Recurring Subscriptions:**

- Monthly community membership

- Quarterly payment plans
- Automatic renewal with easy cancellation

**Payment Plans:**

- 3-pay option (split over 3 months)
- 6-pay option for high-ticket
- Interest-free financing

## 6.7 Revenue Optimization

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

**Testing Focus Areas:**

- Landing page design and copy
- Pricing and packaging
- Call-to-action placement
- Sales page elements
- Checkout flow

**Testing Methodology:**

- A/B testing significant traffic
- Multivariate for complex pages
- User testing and feedback
- Heatmaps and session recording

### Average Order Value (AOV) Increase

**Tactics:**

- Order bumps (add-ons at checkout)
- Upsells (premium versions)
- Cross-sells (complementary products)
- Bundles and packages
- Volume discounts

### Retention & Churn Reduction

**Strategies:**

- Onboarding excellence
- Regular value delivery
- Community engagement
- Win-back campaigns
- Exit surveys and improvements

## 6.8 Financial Management

### Revenue Allocation

**Cost of Goods Sold (10-30%):**

- Payment processing fees (3%)
- Platform fees (5-10%)
- Content production costs (5-15%)
- Product costs (if physical)

**Operating Expenses (40-60%):**

- Team and contractors (25-35%)
- Marketing and ads (10-20%)
- Technology and tools (5-10%)
- Office and operations (5%)

**Profit & Reinvestment (20-40%):**

- Owner compensation
- Tax reserves (25-30% of profit)
- Business reserves (3-6 months runway)
- Growth investment

### Financial Metrics

**Monthly Tracking:**

- Revenue by source
- Expenses by category
- Profit margin
- Cash flow

**Quarterly Review:**

- Revenue growth rate
- Customer acquisition trends
- Retention and churn
- Unit economics health
