## 7.1 Core Team Roles

### Phase 1: Launch (0-$15k MRR)

**Founder/Creative Director (1 FT):**

- Responsibilities:
  - Brand strategy and vision
  - On-camera talent and voice
  - Content creation (writing, video)
  - Community leadership
  - Business development
- Skills Required:
  - Style and aesthetic expertise
  - On-camera presence
  - Writing ability
  - Business acumen
- Compensation: Equity + profit distribution

**Content Producer (0.25-0.5 FTE, Contract):**

- Responsibilities:
  - Video editing
  - Asset organization
  - Publishing and scheduling
  - Basic analytics
- Skills Required:
  - Video editing (Premiere, Final Cut, CapCut)
  - Understanding of social media formats
  - Attention to detail
- Compensation: $25-40/hour or $2,000-4,000/month

**Community Manager (0.25 FTE, Contract):**

- Responsibilities:
  - Daily community engagement
  - Member onboarding
  - Basic moderation
  - Event coordination
- Skills Required:
  - Social media management
  - Customer service
  - Brand alignment
- Compensation: $20-30/hour or $1,500-3,000/month

**Total Team Cost:** $3,500-7,000/month

---

## 7.7 Funding Strategy

### Bootstrap Approach

**Advantages:**

- Full control and ownership
- Forced discipline and efficiency
- No dilution or debt

**Requirements:**

- Personal savings or revenue from existing business
- Lower initial burn rate
- Longer timeline to scale

**Recommended Reserve:** 6-12 months of operating expenses

---

### Revenue Financing

**Approach:**

- Launch with minimal investment
- Use early revenue to fund growth
- Reinvest profits into team and marketing

**Timeline:**

- Month 1-6: Founder-led, minimal costs
- Month 7-18: Add contractors as revenue grows
- Month 19+: Build full team from profits

---

### Outside Capital

**Sources:**

- Angel investors
- Creator economy VCs
- Revenue-based financing
- Small business loans

**Use of Funds:**

- Accelerated team building
- Marketing and paid acquisition
- Technology and systems
- Runway for product development

**Typical Raise:** $250,000-1,000,000 seed round
