## 2.2 Video Content Strategy

**Strategic Role:**

- Primary audience acquisition channel
- Leverage algorithmic distribution for reach
- Demonstrate brand aesthetic and positioning

### Distribution Channels

**Short-Form (Daily Cadence):**

- Platforms: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- Content Themes:
  - Style education and practical application
  - Archetype analysis and cultural commentary
  - Visual storytelling and aesthetic demonstration
- Target Length: 15-90 seconds
- Production Volume: 7-14 per week

**Long-Form (Weekly Cadence):**

- Platform: YouTube
- Content Themes:
  - Cultural analysis and trend commentary
  - Lifestyle philosophy and masculine identity
  - Taste-making and aesthetic education
- Target Length: 8-20 minutes
- Production Volume: 1-2 per week

### Content Formats

**Educational:**

- Style breakdowns and analysis
- How-to guides and tutorials
- Product reviews and recommendations

**Commentary:**

- Cultural trends and analysis
- Archetype explorations
- Philosophical discussions

**Lifestyle:**

- Day-in-the-life content
- Behind-the-scenes
- Event coverage

### Production Framework

**Batch Production:**

- Monthly production days (5-10 videos per session)
- Location scouting and setup
- Multiple outfit/setting changes
- Efficient workflow optimization

**Technology Stack:**

- CapCut: Mobile editing and effects
- Descript: Transcription and editing
- Runway ML: AI-enhanced production
- Voice synthesis for scalability
- B-roll libraries and generation

**Quality Standards:**

- 4K minimum resolution
- Professional lighting and audio
- On-brand color grading
- Consistent visual style

## 2.3 Visual Content Strategy

**Strategic Role:**

- Brand equity and perceived value
- Aspirational positioning
- Cultural authority signaling

### Distribution Channels

**Instagram:**

- Grid: Visual brand showcase, high-quality curated posts
- Stories: Behind-the-scenes, engagement, polls, Q&A
- Reels: Short-form video content
- Posting Cadence: 3-5 grid posts/week, daily stories

**Pinterest:**

- SEO-optimized boards for discovery
- Style inspiration and moodboards
- Product curation
- Drives traffic to owned platforms

**X/Twitter:**

- Thread enhancement with visuals
- Quick style takes and comparisons
- Amplification of other content

### Asset Categories

- Lookbook creation

**Product Curation:**

- Price point breakdowns

**Mood Boards & Collages:**

- Cultural reference collections

**Lifestyle Content:**

## 2.4 Written Content Strategy

**Strategic Role:**

- Thought leadership and intellectual positioning
- Framework development and memetic content
- Audience ideology and worldview formation

### Distribution Channels

**X/Twitter (Daily):**

- Micro-content and aphorisms
- Provocative takes and commentary
- Thread development
- Community engagement

**Substack/Newsletter (Weekly):**

- Long-form essays (1,500-3,000 words)
- Framework development
- Deep cultural analysis
- Subscriber-exclusive content

**Supporting Content:**

- Video scripts (repurposed as standalone content)
- Social media captions
- Image descriptions and context
- Email sequences

### Content Frameworks

**Essays:**

- "Why Modern Men Are Spiritually Homeless"
- "The Death of Casual: A Case for Intentionality"
- "Taste as Power: The New Status Signaling"
- Cultural analysis and trend commentary

**Threads:**

- "10 Style Principles That Never Change"
- "How to Build a $500 Wardrobe That Looks $5,000"
- "Masculine Archetypes and How to Embody Them"
- Listicles and actionable frameworks

**Manifestos:**

- Brand positioning documents
- Worldview articulation
- Community values
- Movement definition

**Premium Playbooks:**

- "Becoming the 1% Archetype" ($97)
- "The Modern Gentleman's Style System" ($197)
- "Status Signaling in the Digital Age" ($147)
- High-value PDF/Notion guides

### Production Framework

**Content Development:**

- Weekly ideation sessions
- Zettelkasten note-taking system
- Obsidian for knowledge management
- Idea capture and organization

**Writing Process:**

- AI-assisted drafting (GPT/Claude)
- Human editing and refinement
- Voice consistency check
- SEO optimization

**Repurposing Workflow:**

- Essay → Thread → Video script
- Thread → Carousel post → Email
- Video → Blog post → Newsletter
- Maximum content leverage
