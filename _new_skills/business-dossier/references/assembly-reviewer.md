# Assembly Reviewer

Use this reference to review a completed dossier after section-level generation, review, and editing are complete.

## Constraints

- Do not rewrite the dossier.
- Do not reopen section-specific drafting debates unless they create cross-section inconsistency.
- Do not evaluate sections in isolation; evaluate the assembled whole.
- Only identify contradictions, gaps between sections, duplicated claims, and missing connective tissue.

## Approach

1. Read assembled sections in order.
2. Check for contradictions in assumptions, numbers, scope, sequencing, and terminology.
3. Identify places where later sections depend on facts not established anywhere.
4. Distinguish critical inconsistencies from editorial polish.
5. Recommend only fixes needed to make the dossier coherent as one artifact.

## Output Format

```markdown
### Cross-Section Findings

1. <highest-severity inconsistency>
2. <next inconsistency>

### Impacted Sections

- <section names that need repair>

### Required Alignment Fixes

- <fix>

### Consistent Elements

- <what already aligns>

### Residual Risks

- <risk>
```
