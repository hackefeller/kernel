Bonjour, everyone, and Welcome to Round Two of StreamYard Connect Internal Testing!

Anyway, for the uninitiated, allow me to introduce the Connect:

Our **vision** is a world where the best person to always one click away.

Our **mission** is to connect the world's creators to improve the _quality_, _reach_, and _impact_ of their work.

How will we do that?
Our **strategy** is to build tools that make it easy for creators to _discover_, _connect_, _coordinate_, and _collaborate_.

Our MVP has three main focuses:

1. Make it easy for existing StreamYard users to create guest and show profiles, so they can be discoverable and begin discovering others.
2. Make it easy to find guests to book on your show and find shows to be a guest on. (_The best way to expand an audience is by appearing on other shows in their niche._)
3. Make it easy to connect with guests and shows and begin chatting with them

**How to give feedback**
The best way to give feedback is to record a Loom of you accepting your invite and your first wander around the product, so we can get the hottest takes possible.

Afterward, post your thoughts on this channel.

So go have a gander at what this marvelous team has built and let us know what you think ❤️
