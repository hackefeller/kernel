# Product Slide Deck

_**Note:** When preparing this document, consider what your customer is thinking. The **customer** in this case is a potential investor._

## Slide 1:

_Should I care about this business?_

- _Does this business have something I want?_
- Title: Brand name
- Subtitle: Outcome + special sauce
  - _Helping millions of people fix their insomnia with skills, not pills._
- _Is this person legit?_

## Slide 2: Credibility

- Introduce yourself and your accomplishments. Convince the investor why you're the person they should be working with.

## Slide 3: Story

- Describe the problem
  - _How big is the problem?_
  - Personal story
    - _Why is this problem significant to the founder?_
    - _Why does the founder have special knowledge about this problem?_
  - _How are customers currently solving this problem?_
  - _Why does this solution suck, and why do customers think this solution sucks?_
  - _Use large numbers, images_

## Slide 4: Introduce your product

- _The investor's primary concern is a significant return on investment, rather than the technical aspects of the solution._
- _Try to compare to existing companies, along with their valuations. This anchors the message to something they understand. ("We're building little league baseball for Fortnite")_
- _Take something understand and show how your solution will be big_

## _Slide 5: Why now?_

- _Why has no one done this?_
- _Why was this not feasible before?_
- _What trends are occurring that make this_

## _Slide 6: Why you?_

- _What is your track record?_
- _What is your credibility?_
- _Slide 7: Money_
  - _How will the business earn revenue?_
  - _How much does it cost to produce the good or provide the service?_
  - _How much does it cost consumers?_
  - _What is the breakeven time?_

## _Slide 8: Investment money_

- _What will you do with the investment?_
  - _List at least three actions and how much each will cost._
- _What goals will the business hit with this investment?_
- _Why are these goals meaningful?_

## Target Market

_Who is the target market for this product?_

_What are their decision factors at every stage of their flow?_
