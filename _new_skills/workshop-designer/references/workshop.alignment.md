# Alignment Workshop

**Duration:** Half day (3-4 hours)\
**Best for:** Teams in conflict or misalignment\
**Investment:** $5,000 - $8,000

---

## Overview

Sometimes the problem isn't the problem—it's that everyone sees the problem differently.

Is your team stuck debating the same issues? Do people seem to have different ideas about priorities and direction? This workshop creates alignment by surfacing disagreements, finding common ground, and establishing frameworks for making decisions together.

We'll facilitate difficult conversations in a structured way that leads to resolution, not more conflict.

---

## What We Do

### Perspective Gathering

Everyone shares their view in a structured format that ensures all voices are heard.

### Finding Common Ground

What do we agree on? Start with shared understanding before tackling differences.

### Surfacing Disagreements

Where do we differ and why? Make implicit conflicts explicit so they can be addressed.

### Resolution Frameworks

How do we decide? Establish clear decision-making processes the team commits to.

### Commitment Building

Moving forward together with shared ownership of decisions.

## Workshop Agenda

### Session (3-4 hours)

**9:00 - 9:20:** Kickoff

- Ground rules for respectful dialogue
- Workshop objectives
- Confidentiality agreements

**9:20 - 10:00:** Individual Perspective Sharing

- Silent writing exercise
- Structured sharing (everyone speaks)
- Active listening (no rebuttals yet)

**10:00 - 10:15:** Break

**10:15 - 11:00:** Finding Common Ground

- Identify areas of agreement
- Shared values and goals
- What's working well

**11:00 - 11:45:** Surfacing & Exploring Disagreements

- Name the conflicts explicitly
- Understand underlying concerns
- Structured debate on key issues
- Identify root causes of disagreement

**11:45 - 12:15:** Resolution & Decision-Making

- Explore potential resolutions
- Decision-making framework
- Voting or consensus (depending on context)
- Document decisions

**12:15 - 12:45:** Commitment & Next Steps

- Public commitments from each person
- Action items with owners
- How we'll work together going forward
- Follow-up plan

**12:45 - 1:00:** Debrief

- Reflect on the process
- Appreciate progress made
- Clarify communication going forward

## Our Approach to Conflict

**Conflicts are normal and healthy**\
Smart people see things differently. That's okay.

**Make it explicit**\
Hidden conflicts are toxic. Named conflicts can be resolved.

**Assume good intent**\
Everyone wants what's best. They just have different views on how to get there.

**Focus on interests, not positions**\
Understand why people want what they want.

**Create decision frameworks**\
It's not about winning arguments, it's about having a process everyone trusts.
