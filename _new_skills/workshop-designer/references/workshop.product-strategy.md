# Product Strategy Workshop

**Duration:** Full day (6-8 hours)\
**Best for:** Product teams at a crossroads or starting something new\
**Investment:** $10,000 - $15,000

---

## Overview

When you need clarity on what to build and why.

Stop guessing about what features to build next. This intensive workshop helps product teams gain strategic clarity on their direction, prioritize ruthlessly, and create a roadmap everyone can rally behind.

We'll work through your market position, competitive landscape, user needs, and feature priorities to create a concrete plan that moves your product forward with confidence.

---

## What We Do

### Market & Competitive Positioning

Where do you fit in the landscape? We'll analyze your competitors, identify white space, and define your unique position in the market.

### Value Proposition Development

What makes you undeniably compelling? We'll refine your core value proposition until it's crystal clear and differentiated.

### User Persona Deep-Dive

Who are we really building for? We'll create detailed personas based on real user data and insights, not assumptions.

### Feature Prioritization

What ships now vs. later? Using impact/effort mapping and forced ranking, we'll make the tough priority decisions.

### Product Roadmap Creation

A 6-12 month plan everyone can rally behind. Clear, actionable, and grounded in strategy.

---

## Facilitation Techniques

**How Might We Exercises**\
Reframe challenges as opportunities to unlock creative problem-solving.

**Forced Ranking**\
Make tough priority decisions by forcing choices between competing options.

**Impact/Effort Mapping**\
Identify quick wins and strategic bets by plotting initiatives on impact vs. effort.

**Jobs-to-be-Done Analysis**\
Understand what users are truly trying to accomplish, not just what they say they want.

---

## Deliverables

### Prioritized Feature Roadmap

A clear, timebound plan for the next 6-12 months with features ranked by strategic value.

One-sentence clarity on who you serve, what problem you solve, and what makes you different.

Detailed persona documents that feel like real people, not generic templates.

No more second-guessing. You'll have strategic clarity and conviction.

## Workshop Agenda

### Morning Session (3-4 hours)

**9:00 - 9:30:** Kickoff & Alignment

- Introductions and context setting
- Define success for the day
- Share constraints and assumptions

**9:30 - 10:30:** Market & Competitive Analysis

- Current market landscape
- Competitor positioning exercise
- Identify white space opportunities

**10:30 - 11:00:** Break

**11:00 - 12:30:** User Research & Personas

- Review existing user insights
- Jobs-to-be-done mapping
- Persona development workshop

**12:30 - 1:30:** Lunch Break

### Afternoon Session (3-4 hours)

**1:30 - 2:30:** Value Proposition Workshop

- Articulate core value proposition
- Test against alternatives
- Refine until compelling

**2:30 - 3:30:** Feature Prioritization

- Brain dump all potential features
- Impact/effort mapping
- Forced ranking exercises

**3:30 - 3:45:** Break

**3:45 - 5:00:** Roadmap Development

- Create 6-12 month roadmap
- Assign rough timelines
- Identify dependencies and risks

**5:00 - 5:30:** Wrap-up & Next Steps

- Review all outputs
- Clarify ownership
- Define follow-up actions
