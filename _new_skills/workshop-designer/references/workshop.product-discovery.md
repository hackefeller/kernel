# Discovery Workshop

**Duration:** Half day (3-4 hours)\
**Best for:** Starting a project or kicking off an engagement\
**Investment:** $5,000 - $8,000

---

## Overview

Before we build anything, we need to understand everything.

Starting a new project? Kicking off a client engagement? This half-day workshop ensures everyone is aligned on the problem, scope, success criteria, and risks before you invest time and money building.

We'll surface hidden assumptions, identify risks early, and create a shared understanding that prevents costly misalignment down the road.

---

## What We Do

### Stakeholder Interviews

Uncover hidden assumptions and constraints through structured conversations with all key players.

### Problem Space Mapping

What are we really solving? Get beyond symptoms to root causes.

### Success Metrics Definition

How do we measure winning? Define concrete, measurable success criteria.

### Risk Identification

What could go wrong? Surface concerns early when they're easier to address.

### Scope Definition

What's in, what's out, what's later? Draw clear boundaries to prevent scope creep.

---

## Facilitation Techniques

**Five Whys**\
Get to root causes by asking "why" repeatedly until you find the real problem.

**Risk Mapping**\
Surface concerns early by mapping likelihood vs. impact of potential issues.

**Assumption Testing**\
Challenge conventional wisdom and document what needs validation.

**Stakeholder Mapping**\
Understand politics, priorities, and decision-making authority.

---

## Deliverables

### Shared Understanding of the Problem

Everyone leaves with the same understanding of what we're solving and why.

### Defined Success Criteria

Concrete, measurable goals that tell us when we've succeeded.

### Documented Assumptions to Test

Clear list of what we believe to be true but need to validate.

### Clear Project Scope

Boundaries that prevent scope creep and misalignment.

### Identified Risks & Mitigation Strategies

Known risks with plans to address or monitor them.

## Workshop Agenda

### Session (3-4 hours)

**9:00 - 9:15:** Kickoff

- Introductions
- Workshop goals
- Ground rules

**9:15 - 10:00:** Problem Definition

- What problem are we solving?
- For whom?
- Why now?
- Five Whys exercise

**10:00 - 10:15:** Break

**10:15 - 11:00:** Stakeholder & User Mapping

- Who cares about this?
- Who will use this?
- Who makes decisions?
- Power/interest mapping

**11:00 - 11:30:** Success Criteria

- What does success look like?
- How will we measure it?
- What are the key metrics?

**11:30 - 12:00:** Scope Definition

- What's in scope?
- What's explicitly out of scope?
- What's "later" (backlog)?

**12:00 - 12:30:** Risk Identification

- What could go wrong?
- What are we most uncertain about?
- What assumptions need testing?
- Risk mitigation strategies

**12:30 - 1:00:** Wrap-up & Next Steps

- Review all outputs
- Clarify ownership
- Define immediate next actions
