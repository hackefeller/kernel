# Workshops That Actually Work

Stop the endless meetings. Skip the theoretical frameworks. Our workshops are where teams align, breakthroughs happen, and real work gets done.

We've spent years refining these sessions with startups and enterprise teams alike. They're designed to move fast, surface truth, and create artifacts you'll actually use. No corporate theater. No trust falls. Just focused, productive collaboration that moves your product forward.

---

## Our Workshop Philosophy

**Time is sacred.** We don't waste it. Every workshop is designed to produce tangible outcomes in hours, not days.

**Disagreement is data.** The best insights emerge when smart people see things differently. We facilitate productive conflict, not artificial consensus.

**Making > Talking.** You'll spend more time creating than discussing. Prototypes beat PowerPoints every time.

**Real problems only.** We work on your actual challenges, not hypothetical case studies.

## Workshop Offerings

### [Product Strategy Workshop](workshop.product-strategy.md)

**Duration:** Full day (6-8 hours) | **Investment:** $10K - $15K

When you need clarity on what to build and why. We'll work through market positioning, value proposition, user personas, feature prioritization, and create a 6-12 month roadmap everyone can rally behind.

**Perfect for:** Product teams at a crossroads, products struggling with feature bloat, teams with competing visions, founders preparing to scale.

---

### [Product Design Workshop](workshop.product-design.md)

**Duration:** 1-2 days | **Investment:** $10K - $25K

Transform fuzzy ideas into concrete designs that you can test with real users. Using techniques like Crazy 8's and rapid prototyping, we'll go from concept to clickable prototype in days, not weeks.

**Perfect for:** New features that need design direction, redesigns of existing products, complex UX challenges, cross-functional team alignment.

---

### [Discovery Workshop](workshop.product-discovery.md)

**Duration:** Half day (3-4 hours) | **Investment:** $5K - $8K

Before we build anything, we need to understand everything. This workshop surfaces hidden assumptions, identifies risks, defines success criteria, and creates shared understanding across stakeholders.

**Perfect for:** Project kickoffs, new client engagements, strategic planning sessions, teams with misaligned expectations.

---

### [Design Sprint](workshop.design-sprint.md)

**Duration:** 5 days (Monday-Friday) | **Investment:** $25K - $40K

Test big ideas without big commitments. Based on Google Ventures methodology, this 5-day sprint takes you from challenge to prototype to real user feedback. By Friday, you'll know if your idea is worth building.

**Perfect for:** Testing big bets before committing resources, breaking through analysis paralysis, getting user feedback fast, building team confidence.

---

### [Technical Architecture Workshop](workshop.technical-architecture.md)

**Duration:** Full day (6-8 hours) | **Investment:** $10K - $15K

Make the right technical decisions before they become technical debt. We'll evaluate tech stacks, design system architecture, plan database schemas, and create technical documentation that guides implementation.

**Perfect for:** Starting new technical projects, planning major refactors, evaluating build vs. buy decisions, onboarding new technical leaders.

---

### [Brand & Positioning Workshop](workshop-brand-positioning.md)

**Duration:** Full day (6-8 hours) | **Investment:** $10K - $15K

Your brand isn't your logo—it's what people think and feel when they hear your name. We'll define your brand essence, competitive differentiation, voice and tone, visual direction, and messaging framework.

**Perfect for:** Early-stage startups finding their identity, rebrands or repositioning efforts, companies entering new markets, organizations with inconsistent messaging.

---

### [Alignment Workshop](workshop.alignment.md)

**Duration:** Half day (3-4 hours) | **Investment:** $5K - $8K

Sometimes the problem isn't the problem—it's that everyone sees it differently. This workshop creates alignment by surfacing disagreements, finding common ground, and establishing frameworks for making decisions together.

**Perfect for:** Leadership team conflicts, product direction disagreements, cross-functional team friction, merger or acquisition integration.

**We're practitioners, not consultants.** We build products every day. These aren't academic exercises—they're battle-tested processes we use ourselves.

**We facilitate, not dictate.** The answers are in the room. We create the structure for your team to find them.

- **On-site** — We come to you (best for team bonding and focus)
