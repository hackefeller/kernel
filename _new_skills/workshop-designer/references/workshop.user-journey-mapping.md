**_What is user journey mapping?_**
User journey mapping is a process of laying out the steps customers take to achieve their goal using the product and customers' touchpoints and feelings during this process.

**What is the purpose of user journey mapping?**

- Identify gaps in the customer experience.
- Discover new opportunities

## **Steps**

- &nbsp;
  - _Identify the primary persona_ - guests and shows
    - How would we describe the product's ideal guest?
    - How would we describe the product's ideal show?

## The Map

- **Awareness** - How can users learn that Collab exists?
- **Research** -
  - What do users want to hear when they arrive on the product's landing page?
- **Selection** - how do users choose to use our product?
- **Delivery** - Once our user has signed up, how do they experience the initial use?
- **Followup** - How do we retain customers and be an advocate

- **Doing** - What actions do customers take at each stage?
- **Thinking** - What emotions and thoughts do they have at each stage?
- **Touchpoints** - What surfaces will users interact with at each stage?
- **Pains** - What frustrations do users experience during each stage?
- **Opportunities** - What innovative solutions can solve each pain point?
