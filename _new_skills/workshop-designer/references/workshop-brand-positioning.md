# Brand & Positioning Workshop

**Duration:** Full day (6-8 hours)\
**Best for:** Companies finding their voice and differentiation\
**Investment:** $10,000 - $15,000

---

## Overview

Your brand isn't your logo. It's what people think and feel when they hear your name.

Are you saying the same things as your competitors? Does your brand feel generic? Are different team members representing you differently? This workshop creates clarity on who you are, what makes you different, and how you show up consistently everywhere.

We'll dig deep into your brand essence, position you against competitors, define your voice, and create guidelines everyone can follow.

---

## What We Do

### Brand Essence Exploration

Who are you, really? Beyond what you sell, what do you stand for?

### Competitor Differentiation

What makes you different (and provable)? We'll identify your unique position in the market.

### Voice & Tone Definition

How should you sound? Playful or serious? Formal or casual? We'll define your brand voice.

### Visual Identity Direction

Early explorations of how you look. Color palettes, typography, imagery style.

### Messaging Framework

What you say, to whom, when. Structured messaging for different audiences and contexts.

---

## Facilitation Techniques

**Brand Personality Mapping**\
Define your brand as a person with personality traits, values, and quirks.

**Competitor Perception Analysis**\
How are competitors perceived? Where's the white space?

**Voice Spectrum Exercises**\
Place your brand on spectrums (formal ↔ casual, serious ↔ playful, etc.)

**Messaging Hierarchy Development**\
Organize messages from core positioning to specific feature benefits.

---

## Deliverables

### Brand Positioning Statement
